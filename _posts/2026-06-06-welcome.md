## Key concepts

Every entry is a Markdown file in `_posts/`, named `YYYY-MM-DD-topic-slug.md`.
The frontmatter at the top (the block between the `---` lines) carries the
title, author, date, description, and tags. The body follows a fixed set of
sections so entries stay scannable: Summary, Key concepts, Code, Gotchas, Open
questions, References.

The `description` and `tags` are worth a moment of care — they show up in search
results and shape how findable an entry is later.
